This vanilla cake is a must have recipe in your repertoire. It is light, fluffy and full of vanilla flavor. This recipe can be complemented by any filling of your choice and as it is not overly sweet it can be enjoyed without being overly sweet. This recipe uses pantry staples which makes it even easier to make.

    Ingredients:
  • Flour - 2 cups
  • Baking Powder - 1 1/2 tsp
  • Salt - 1/8 tsp
  • Butter - 1/2 cup
  • Sugar - 1 cup
  • Vanilla Extract - 1 tsp
  • Eggs - 2
  • Milk - 3/4 cup
    Procedure:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degree celcius.
  2. Combine the flour, baking powder in a medium bowl and sift it to remove any impurities.
  3. In a large bowl, beat the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. It takes about 5 minutes.
  4. Add salt and vanilla to the butter mix and mix until well combined.
