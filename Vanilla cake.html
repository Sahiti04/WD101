This vanilla cake is a must have recipe in your repertoire. It is light, fluffy and full of vanilla flavor. This recipe can be complemented by any filling of your choice and as it is not overly sweet it can be enjoyed without being overly sweet. This recipe uses pantry staples which makes it even easier to make.

    Ingredients:
  • Flour - 2 cups
  • Baking Powder - 1 1/2 tsp
  • Salt - 1/8 tsp
  • Butter - 1/2 cup
  • Sugar - 1 cup
  • Vanilla Extract - 1 tsp
  • Eggs - 2
  • Milk - 3/4 cup
    Procedure:
  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degree celcius.
  2. Combine the flour, baking powder in a medium bowl and sift it to remove any impurities.
  3. In a large bowl, beat the butter and sugar together until light and fluffy. It takes about 5 minutes.
  4. Add salt and vanilla to the butter mix and mix until well combined.
  5. Separate the egg whites and yolks. Add the yolks to the milk and mix them together. Whip the egg whites until they get light and fluffy.
  6. Add the milk mixture to the butter mixture and combine them.
  7. Mix the wet ingredients and the dry ingredients. After they become a nice homogenous mix gently fold in the whipped egg whites.
  8. Pour the batter in a greased pan and cook it for about 20 minutes. But as every oven is different, start checking the cake after 15 minutes. To test the doneness, you can insert a toothpick into the centre of the cake. If the toothpick comes out clean, it's done.
