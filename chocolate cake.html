This is my recipe for a moist chocolate cake. It is easy to make and requires less tools to make. The ingredients are pantry staples so this makes it even easier.

    Ingredients:

  • Flour - 2 1/4 cups
  • Sugar - 1 1/2 cups
  • Cocoa Powder - 1/2 cup
  • Vegetable Oil - 1/2 cup
  • Water - 3/4 cup
  • Milk - 3/4 cup
  • Baking Soda - 1 1/2 tsp
  • Salt - 1/4 tsp
  • Vanilla Extract - 1 1/2 tsp
  • Instant coffee powder - 1 tsp
