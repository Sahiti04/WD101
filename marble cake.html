Marble Cake

This is my recipe for a marble cake. It is so light, fluffy and delicious. It is made without eggs and it can be completely vegan too. This recipe can be made with or without an oven.

Note:

This recipe can be made vegan by substituting the milk with the one of your choice.

    Ingredients:

  • Flour - 2 cups
  • Cocoa Powder - 3 tbsp
  • Baking Powder - 1 tsp
  • Baking Soda - 1/2 tsp
  • Sugar - 1 cup
  • Yogurt - 1 cup
  • Oil - 3/4 cup
  • Milk - 4 tbsp
  • Salt - a pinch

    Procedure:

  1. First start by combining flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt. Sift them to get rid of any impurities.
  2. Then combine the Yogurt and sugar until the sugar dissolves.
  3. After the sugar dissolves, add the oil and mix until well combined.
  4. Now add the flour mix and until they form a homogeneous mixture.
  5. Add two tablespoons of milk and mix it in.
  6. After mixing the milk, divide the batter into two equal parts.
  7. In one part add the cocoa powder and two more tablespoons of milk and mix well.
  8. Alternate adding a spoon of each mixture in a greased tin until you run out of batter.
  9. Bake it in a 180 degree preheated oven for about 20 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
